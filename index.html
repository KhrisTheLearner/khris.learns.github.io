About

Growing up in Rwanda, I witnessed how language shapes opportunity. While global AI advances at breakneck speed, my native Kinyarwanda‚Äîspoken by 12 million people‚Äîremains largely invisible to these technologies. That's what drives my research: ensuring that African languages aren't left behind in the AI revolution.

I specialize in cross-lingual transfer learning for low-resource African languages. My thesis explores how morphology-aware NLP methods can improve educational assessment tools for Kinyarwanda, directly supporting Rwanda's Vision 2050 educational digitalization goals.

Beyond the technical challenges, I'm deeply invested in building sustainable solutions‚Äîcreating employment opportunities for Rwandan language specialists and establishing infrastructure that will outlast any single research project. I believe the best technology serves people, not the other way around.
